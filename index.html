About Jay’s KickAsh BBQ

Smoked low and slow, served hot and fast.

Ponca City, Oklahoma

Jay’s KickAsh BBQ is a local food truck bringing legit smoked meat to Ponca City and the surrounding area. Everything is cooked low and slow so you get deep smoke flavor, tender meat, and all the KickAsh sides to go with it.

Whether you are in the mood for sliced brisket, ribs, or that old-school smoked bologna, we have you covered. Grab a sandwich, load up some fries or tots, and wash it down with an ice-cold drink.

Signature Items

  • Smoked Brisket Sandwich
  • Fall-off-the-bone Ribs
  • Smoked Bologna Sandwich
  • Loaded Fries or Tots with Brisket or Pulled Pork
