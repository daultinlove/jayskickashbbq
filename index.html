About Jay’s KickAsh BBQ

Smoked low and slow, served hot and fast.

Ponca City, Oklahoma

Jay’s KickAsh BBQ is a local food truck bringing legit smoked meat to Ponca City and the surrounding area. Everything is cooked low and slow for deep smoke flavor and tender meat.

From sliced brisket and ribs to old-school smoked bologna, we’ve got you covered. Grab a sandwich, load up some fries or tots, and enjoy.

Signature Items

  • Smoked Brisket or Pulled Pork Sandwich
  • Fall-off-the-bone Ribs
  • Smoked Bologna Sandwich
  • Loaded Fries or Tots
